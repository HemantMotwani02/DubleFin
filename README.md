# DubleFin

DubleFin is a financial management application designed to help users manage their accounts, track transactions, and stay informed with notifications. The platform provides a seamless experience for both web and mobile users, making it easy to register, log in, and access your financial data securely.

## Key Features
- **User Registration & Login:** Securely create an account and access your dashboard.
- **Account Management:** View and manage your financial accounts in one place.
- **Transaction Tracking:** Easily add, view, and monitor your transactions.
- **Notifications:** Stay updated with important alerts and notifications.
- **User-Friendly Interface:** Simple and intuitive design for all users.

## How to Use
1. **Sign Up:** Create your account using the registration page.
2. **Log In:** Access your dashboard with your credentials.
3. **Manage Finances:** Add accounts, track transactions, and receive notifications.

## About
DubleFin is built to make personal finance management easy and accessible for everyone. Whether you are an individual or a small business, DubleFin helps you keep your finances organized and up to date.
